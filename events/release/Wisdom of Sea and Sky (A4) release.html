Wisdom of Sea and Sky (A4) release

Release date
Jul 30, 2025 - Ongoing

Wisdom of Sea and Sky (A4) release is now available! This expansion introduces new ex cards and exclusive rewards.

New cards from this set can be obtained by opening Wisdom of Sea and Sky (A4) booster packs. Complete missions to earn Emblem Tickets and special items!

Game Updates

  • New solo step-up Battles (see below)
  • Trade tokens are exchangeable for Pack Hourglasses and Shinedust. They will no longer be used to trade cards. Instead, for trades involving cards of rarity and , Shinedust will be used. More information on our trading guide here.
  • A wishlist feature is now available in "My cards".
  • Cards from Eevee Grove (A3b) become available for trading.
  • Multiple quality of life changes
