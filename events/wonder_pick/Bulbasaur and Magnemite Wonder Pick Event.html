Bulbasaur and Magnemite Wonder Pick Event

Event period
Dec 6 - Dec 27, 2024

A new Wonder Pick event is now live. Players can obtain Promo cards—Magnemite and Bulbasaur—through Bonus Picks and Chansey Picks.

In addition, new themed accessories have been added to the shop. These items can be exchanged using Event Shop Tickets, which can be earned by completing event missions and participating in Bonus Picks.

Don't miss this limited-time opportunity to collect exclusive promo cards and special accessories.

Wonder Pick

Event period
Dec 6 - Dec 27, 2024

During the event period, 'Chansey' and 'Bonus Pick' will appear in the Get Challenge. By clearing these challenges, you can obtain the event-exclusive promo cards Magnemite and Bulbasaur.

The timing of challenge appearances varies by user and occurs completely at random. Therefore, we recommend that you regularly open the app during the event period to check if challenges have appeared.

Bonus Pick

Free Challenges can be attempted without consuming any Challenge Power. Be sure to take them on whenever you see them.

Chansey Pick

Use 2 Wonder Stamina to attempt the Chansey Pick. Save 2 during the event so you're always ready when it appears.
