PROMO Reissue Wonder Pick Event 2025

Event period
Oct 30 - Nov 21, 2025

This event gives you another chance to obtain promotional cards from previous Wonder Pick events.

All cards released in the A Series Wonder Pick events are available again for a limited time.

During the event, you can try the free Bonus Pick, where you can earn valuable rewards such as Shop Tickets and Wonder Hourglasses. In addition, the Chansey Pick offers a chance to obtain these past promotional cards.

You can also use tickets earned by completing missions to redeem one card of your choice from the full lineup of 24 Promo A cards available during the event.

Wonder Pick

Event period
Oct 30 - Nov 21, 2025

During the event period, 'Chansey' and 'Bonus Pick' will appear in the Get Challenge. By clearing these challenges, you can obtain event-exclusive promo cards from previous Wonder Pick events.

The timing of challenge appearances varies by user and occurs completely at random. Therefore, we recommend that you regularly open the app during the event period to check if challenges have appeared.

Bonus Pick

Free Challenges can be attempted without consuming any Challenge Power. Be sure to take them on whenever you see them.

Chansey Pick

Use 2 Wonder Stamina to attempt the Chansey Pick. Save 2 during the event so you're always ready when it appears.
