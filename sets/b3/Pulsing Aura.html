Pulsing Aura Pack is a booster pack in Pulsing Aura (B3). This pack features Fighting-type Pokémon along with Trainer cards that enhance their performance, as well as a variety of cards illustrated in dynamic battle scenes. It includes Mega Evolution Pokémon such as Mega Lucario and Mega Sceptile, highlighting intense and action-focused card designs.
