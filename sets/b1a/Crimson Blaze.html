Crimson Blaze

December 17, 2025

Crimson Blaze (B1A) is an expansion set of Series B in Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, following Mega Rising (B1). This set features powerful Mega Evolution Pokémon such as Mega Charizard Y, alongside strong non-ex Pokémon like Furfrou and Magnezone, which play an important role in the metagame. It also highlights support cards that strengthen evolution lines, particularly those related to Magneton, with characters such as Clemont providing key synergy support.
