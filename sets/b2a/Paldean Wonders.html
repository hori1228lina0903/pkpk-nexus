Paldean Wonders

February 26, 2026

Paldean Wonders (B2A) is an expansion set of Series B in Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, following Fantastical Parade (B2). This set focuses on Pokémon originally discovered in the Paldea region, introducing a wide range of region-themed cards with balanced roles across offense, defense, and utility. It also strengthens Water-type strategies with powerful additions such as Baxcalibur and Chien-Pao ex, significantly enhancing the viability of Water-based decks while expanding synergy through support cards.
