Secluded Springs Pack

August 28, 2025

Secluded Springs Pack is the only booster pack in Secluded Springs (A4a). In this pack, players can obtain Legendary Beast ex cards such as Raikou ex, Entei ex, and Suicune ex, along with Pokémon and Trainer cards from the Johto region. It also includes Mantyke, a Baby Pokémon that supports Water Energy acceleration, along with other cards that improve hand consistency and setup.
