Shining Revelry

March 27, 2025

Shining Revelry (A2b) is a special expansion set for Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, released on March 27, 2025. This set marks the first appearance of Shiny cards, featuring newly introduced Pokémon with color-variant designs. In addition to its visual focus, the set also expands gameplay by introducing more cards and support options that are effective against Pokémon ex, adding new ways to counter high-power cards. While it increases variety, some players have expressed concerns about the future direction of Shiny-focused content.
