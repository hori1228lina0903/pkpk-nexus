Lugia Pack

July 30, 2025

Lugia Pack is one of the booster packs in Wisdom of Sea and Sky (A4). It mainly includes Water-type Pokémon along with Trainer cards that improve consistency and speed. In this pack, players can obtain cards like Natsuki, which helps stabilize coin-flip-based attacks, and Elemental Switch, which allows faster energy movement and makes Lightning-type decks more aggressive.
