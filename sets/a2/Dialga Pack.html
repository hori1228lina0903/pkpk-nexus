Dialga Pack

January 29, 2025

Dialga Pack is one of the two booster packs in Space-Time Smackdown (A2). It mainly includes Steel-type Pokémon and cards with strong passive abilities, along with a higher number of Trainer and Tool cards. Notable examples include Pokémon like Lucario and Darkrai, which highlight the pack’s focus on ability-based support and consistent setups.
