Deck Features

  • Writhe down the opponent with Darkrai ex's Ability by dealing a continuous 20 damage before finishing them off with Giratina ex's high-output 130 damage.
  • Enhance durability for long-term battles by utilizing Primordial Plains and Indeedee ex for HP recovery, while using Rocky Helmet to reflect damage back at high-power attackers.
  • While it is a slower, control-oriented build vulnerable to ultra-fast aggro, its strength lies in its stability and ability to gain an advantage against a wide range of ex decks.

Key Card Analysis

Giratina ex
  • The deck's primary attacker.
  • Self-accelerates Psychic Energy at the end of your turn with its "Abyssal Cry" Ability.
  • Can unleash "Chaos Impact" for 130 damage consecutively from the 3rd turn to secure one-hit KOs on opponent ex cards.
Darkrai ex
  • Plays a supporting chip-damage role.
  • Deals 20 damage to the opponent's Active Pokémon whenever a Darkness Energy is attached to itself via its Ability.
  • Softens up targets from the Bench to bring them within Giratina ex's knockout range.
Indeedee ex
  • Heals 20 HP with its Ability to buy more time during the match.
  • Can also heal Benched Pokémon, providing more flexibility in maintaining your board.
Rocky Helmet
  • Deals 20 damage back to the attacker when the holder receives damage.
  • Deters high-power ex decks and provides an advantage in long-term battles.
Lucky Ice Pop
  • Capable of significant healing depending on your luck.
  • If you're lucky, it allows for consecutive healing to maintain the durability of Giratina ex and Indeedee ex.
Starting Plains
  • Provides a +20 HP boost to all Basic Pokémon in this Basic-only deck.
  • Further strengthens the defensive core centered around Indeedee ex and Darkrai ex.

How to Play Giratina ex & Darkrai ex Deck

  1. Deploy Giratina ex or Darkrai ex to the Active Spot and attach Rocky Helmet or Giant Cape to enhance their early-game durability.
  2. On the Bench, use Darkrai ex's Ability to chip away 20 damage from the opponent's Active Pokémon whenever you attach Darkness Energy.
  3. Utilize Indeedee ex's Ability and Starting Plains to boost your HP and recover damage, stalling the opponent while Giratina ex self-accelerates Energy via "Abyssal Cry."
  4. Once setup is complete, use Cyrus or Sabrina to force out high-priority targets and deliver a 130-damage "Chaos Impact" from Giratina ex to secure the KO.
  5. Maintain your board state by using Lucky Ice Pop for consecutive healing, ensuring your ex attackers stay on the field as long as possible in a battle of attrition.
