Shining Revelry (A2b) release: Upcoming events, New items, Solo battles.

Mar 27 - Dec 31

Shining Revelry (A2b) is out! The set features new Shiny Pokémon and rarities and many other exciting new features. You can check the full card list on the expansion page. It comes in a single booster.

Game updates

  • New solo step-up Battles (see below)
  • Cards from Triumphant Light (A2a) become available for trading.
  • The number of decks in the builder has been increased from 15 to 20.
  • Minor qualify of life updates (bigger font on some cards, improvements on effects...)

Shining Revelry events

Shining Revelry will be the main pack for at least 4 weeks. These events will take place while it's the latest released pack:

  • March 28: 1st season of the Ranked Match Mode, with missions to earn rewards. The ex Starter Deck Mission allow you to get a full deck featuring one of the 9 Pokémon ex from Shining Revelry!
  • April 1: The monthly Premium Shop update. The log-in reward is an alternative art of the Sprigatito card from Shining Revelry, and the accessories will be themed around Shiny Charizard, one of the shiny Pokémon in the cover of Shining Revelry.
  • April 1: a gift from the Developer team of 1000 Trade tokens 1000 can be claimed from the Gift box.
  • April 3 (to be confirmed): Sneak Peek campaign: preview one of the card in your Wonder Pick in this event!
  • April 7: Pawmot Drop Event. Get an exclusive art rare version of Pawmot from Shining Revelry and exclusive cards such as Promo Floatzel and Promo Ekans.
  • April 16: Riolu and Pachirisu Wonder Pick Event. A Wonder Pick event where you can get Promo Riolu, Promo Pachirisu and Lucario-themed accessories in exchange of Event Shop Tickets (Lucario)
  • April 28: Fighting-Type Mass Outbreak Wonder Pick Event. themed Wonder Pick event featuring cards from many sets. The Rare Pick gives you a chance to get your hand on the new rare Lucario ex.
  • The next set A3 will be released on April 30.

New shop items

A Giratina emblem purchasable with Emblem Tickets, a Shiny Pokémon cover and a Springtime backdrop purchasable with Shop Tickets and two Red-themed bundles purchasable with Poké Gold are available in the Shop.
