Darkness-Type Mass Outbreak Wonder Pick Event

Feb 21 - Feb 27

Collect Pokémon from Genetic Apex and Mythical Island in this Wonder Pick event! Try your luck at both free Bonus Pick, where you can also collect items such as Pack Hourglasses, and Rare Picks, where you can grab the Weavile ex and other rare cards!

Bonus Pick (free)

There is ten possible combinations of Bonus Pick for this event, each with 10% chance to appear. Bonus Pick have a 15% chance to appear whenever a new pick appears on your screen.

Is the Darkness-Type Mass Outbreak Wonder Pick event good?

There are only benefits to picking from Bonus Picks, either to complete your collection or to even try to get a Shop Ticket or a Pack Hourglass.

For the Pokémon cards that you can get:

  • Weavile ex is one of the main Pokémon of its own archetype. It's a very aggressive deck that can quickly set itself in motion to deal 90 damage every single turn, thanks to the association with Darkrai ex from the Dialga pack and its Nightmare Aura ability combining with Weavile ex's Scratching Nails. You can find our deck guide for Weavile ex here:
