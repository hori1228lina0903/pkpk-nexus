Premium Shop Lineup Update (June 2025)

Jun 1 - Aug 1

Every two months on Pokémon TCG Pocket, the Premium Shop refreshes with a new selection of exclusive accessories. These are exclusively available for a limited time, and only for Premium Pass holders; learn more about the Premium Pass on our dedicated page.

In June, the premium accessories are Alolan Vulpix-themed. The log-in reward this time is a Promo Zeraora, an alternative art of the same card from Extradimensional Crisis.

A new month also brings Refreshed Monthly Hourglasses, which are available for all users.
