Pawmi and Paldean Clodsire Wonder Pick Event

Mar 15 - Apr 1

A new Wonder Pick event with Bonus Picks and Chansey Picks to get a chance at obtaining a new Promo Pawmi and Promo Paldean Clodsire! Both are identical from their print in Paldean Wonders, but with a brand new alternative artwork.

New Arven themed acccessories are also available in the shop. You can use Event Shop Tickets (Arven) to purchase them: to obtain them, complete the event missions and try your luck at the Bonus Picks.

Wonder Pick

Bonus Pick (free)

There are 4 different combinations of Bonus Pick for this event, each with different probabilities to appear. They include Pawmi (Paldean Wonders and Promo B) and Paldean Clodsire (Paldean Wonders and Promo B) cards, Event Shop Tickets and Wonder Hourglasses.

Arven accessories & shop items

You can collect event shop tickets from event missions that you can complete by wonder picking and collecting the event-related cards, as well as from the aforementioned Bonus Pick. The event shop tickets you collect can be exchanged for accessories in the Shop > Limited Time/Events section.

By completing a mission, you can also get a Promo Card Exchange Ticket (Arven Wonder Pick Event) and redeem it for one-of Promo B Pawmi or one-of Promo B Paldean Clodsire.
