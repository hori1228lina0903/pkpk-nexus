Genetic Apex

October 30, 2024

Genetic Apex (A1) is the first expansion set for Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, released on October 30, 2024. This set features three distinct booster packs—Mewtwo, Charizard, and Pikachu—each highlighting different Pokémon lineups and strategies, while introducing foundational Pokémon ex and core Trainer cards that define the game’s initial deck-building environment.
