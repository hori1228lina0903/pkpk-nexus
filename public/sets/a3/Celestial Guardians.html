Celestial Guardians

April 30, 2025

Celestial Guardians (A3) is a major expansion set for Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, released on April 30, 2025. This set introduces Rare Candy, allowing Stage 2 Pokémon to be set up much faster, and adds cards like Oricorio that make all-Pokémon ex builds less reliable. With two themed booster packs—Solgaleo and Lunala—it expands both evolution speed and counterplay options across the format.
