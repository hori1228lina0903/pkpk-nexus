Space-Time Smackdown

January 29, 2025

Space-Time Smackdown (A2) is the second major expansion set for Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, released on January 30, 2025. This set consists of two themed booster packs—Dialga and Palkia—and introduces Pokémon Tool cards, adding new types of effects and expanding the variety of strategies available across different card pools.
