Pulsing Aura (B3) is an expansion set of Series B in Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, following Mega Shine (B2B). This set significantly strengthens Fighting-type Pokémon through newly introduced Trainer cards, improving their consistency and overall performance. It also features many cards illustrated in dynamic battle scenes, including Mega Evolution Pokémon such as Mega Lucario and Mega Sceptile, emphasizing intense and action-driven gameplay.
