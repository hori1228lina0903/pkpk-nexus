Eevee Grove

June 26, 2025

Eevee Grove (A3b) is a special expansion set for Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, released on June 27, 2025. This set features a single booster pack centered on Eevee and its Evolutions, and introduces dedicated Tool cards specifically designed for Eevee, along with a wide range of Evolution Pokémon and supporting Trainer cards. As one of the most popular Pokémon, Eevee is likely to receive further support in future expansions.
