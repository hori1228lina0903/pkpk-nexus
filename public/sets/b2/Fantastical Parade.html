Fantastical Parade

January 29, 2026

Fantastical Parade (B2) is an expansion set of Series B in Pokémon Trading Card Game Pocket, following Crimson Blaze (B1A). This set introduces Stadium cards for the first time, adding new field-based effects that influence the flow of battles. It also features a variety of whimsical and fantasy-themed Pokémon, with an emphasis on strategic versatility and unique card interactions. Certain cards, such as Lucky Ice Pop, introduce high-variance effects that can result in extremely large recovery depending on coin flips.
