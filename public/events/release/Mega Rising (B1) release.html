Mega Rising (B1) release

Release date
Oct 30, 2025 - Ongoing

Mega Rising (B1) release is now available! This expansion introduces new ex cards and exclusive rewards.

New cards from this set can be obtained by opening Mega Rising (B1) booster packs. Complete missions to earn Emblem Tickets and special items!

Game Updates

  • You can now trade cards from all previous sets AND from the current expansion (Mega Rising) as well!
  • You can also now trade cards of the rarity , and !
  • The Share feature is now available! Once per day, share a card of rarity - to one of your friend.
  • You can now automatically collect certain flair if you collect 10 of the same card of rarity up to . To obtain even more flair, you can still do it from the "Obtain Items" menu. From B Series onward, cards will no longer need to be consumed to obtain flair: only Shinedust will.
  • Daily Missions will have more missions, and will also include Wonder Hourglasses as a reward
  • Packs can be favorited.
  • The number of decks has been raised from 20 to 25.
  • The level cap has been raised from 50 to 60.
  • UI improvement.
  • A lottery will be held for Advance Ticket owners, the mysterious ticket given at the very beginning of Pokémon TCG Pocket, with a chance to gain up to 1,200 Pack Hourglasses!
