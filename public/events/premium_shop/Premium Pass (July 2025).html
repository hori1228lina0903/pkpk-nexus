Premium Pass Update (July 2025)

Event period
Jul 1 - Sep 1, 2025

The July 2025 Premium Pass in Pokémon TCG Pocket introduces a new selection of exclusive rewards available in the Premium Shop. These items are only available for a limited time and can be obtained exclusively by players with an active Premium Pass subscription.

One of the highlights of the July 2025 Premium Pass rewards is the Art Rare promo card, which is available as this month’s login reward. Players who log in to Pokémon TCG Pocket while their Premium Pass is active will automatically complete the mission and can claim the promo card as a reward.
