Slowpoke Drop Event

Event period
Apr 9 - Apr 26, 2026

The Slowpoke Drop Event is a limited-time solo battle event. During the event period, you can challenge special battles to obtain promo cards and other rewards. Complete all tasks in each battle to earn bonus rewards!

Promo Pack

Pack name
Promo Pack B Series Vol. 6

These promo cards can be obtained as rewards from event battles.

Event Battles

Event period
Apr 9 - Apr 26, 2026

Clear battles to earn rewards. Higher difficulties offer better rewards.

Beginner Deck: Water

Tasks

  • Knock Out your opponent’s Active Pokémon 1 time with an attack from a Lightning-type Pokémon
  • Put 3 Basic Pokémon into play
Intermediate Deck: Water

Tasks

  • Knock Out your opponent’s Active Pokémon 1 time with an attack from a Lightning-type Pokémon
  • Put a Stage 1 Pokémon into play
  • Win this battle with 2 or more Mega Shine cards included in your deck
Advanced Deck: Water

Tasks

  • Knock Out your opponent’s Active Pokémon 1 time with an attack from a Lightning-type Pokémon
  • Win this battle with 1 or more Mega Evolution Pokémon ex included in your deck
  • Win this battle with 6 or more Mega Shine cards included in your deck
  • Win this battle without your opponent getting any points
