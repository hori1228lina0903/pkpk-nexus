Mega Pidgeot ex Drop event

Event period
Nov 18 - Dec 5, 2025

The Mega Pidgeot ex Drop event is a limited-time solo battle event. During the event period, you can challenge special battles to obtain promo cards and other rewards. Complete all tasks in each battle to earn bonus rewards!

Promo Pack

Pack name
Promo Pack B Series Vol. 1

These promo cards can be obtained as rewards from event battles.

Event Battles

Event period
Nov 18 - Dec 5, 2025

Clear battles to earn rewards. Higher difficulties offer better rewards.

Beginner Deck: Unknown

Tasks

  • Knock Out your opponent’s Active Pokémon 1 time with an attack from a Lightning-type Pokémon
  • Put 3 Basic Pokémon into play
Intermediate Deck: Unknown

Tasks

  • Knock Out your opponent’s Active Pokémon 1 time with an attack from a Lightning-type Pokémon
  • Put a Stage 1 Pokémon into play
  • Win this battle with 2 or more Mega Rising cards included in your deck
Advanced Deck: Unknown

Tasks

  • Knock Out your opponent’s Active Pokémon 1 time with an attack from a Lightning-type Pokémon
  • Win this battle without any Pokémon ex included in your deck
  • Win this battle with 6 or more Mega Rising cards included in your deck
  • Win this battle without your opponent getting any points
