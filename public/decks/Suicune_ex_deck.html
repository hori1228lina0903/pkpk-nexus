  1. In the early game, lead with Suicune ex and use its Ability to draw cards while setting up the board. Use Mantyke and support cards to accelerate Water Energy and prepare the Baxcalibur line.
  2. During the mid game, use Baxcalibur’s Energy acceleration to quickly power up Suicune ex and apply pressure with repeated high-damage attacks.
  3. In the late game, use Baxcalibur’s Energy acceleration to repeatedly use Chien-Pao ex’s attacks and finish the match.

Deck Weaknesses

Since Baxcalibur is a Stage 2 Pokémon, the deck can become slow if the evolution line is not set up consistently.

Because Suicune ex’s damage depends on the Bench, its damage output can drop significantly if the opponent limits Bench size.

Since the deck’s Pokémon generally have lower HP, losing Suicune ex early can put the deck in a very difficult position.
