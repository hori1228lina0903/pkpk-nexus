If the early-game stall from Goomy fails or the evolution line is disrupted, the deck can quickly lose tempo.

Deck Weaknesses

Mega Sceptile ex loses 1 Grass Energy after using its attack, making it vulnerable to Energy disruption and limiting sustained offense.

It is difficult to re-attach Energy efficiently to a second Mega Sceptile ex, leading to resource management issues in longer games.

If the Sceptile evolution line is disrupted early, it becomes difficult to consistently set up Mega Sceptile ex.
