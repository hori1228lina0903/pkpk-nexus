Deck Weaknesses

Magnezone ex requires both evolution setup and multiple Energy attachments, making fast aggressive matchups difficult.

If Magnemite or Magneton are knocked out early, it can become difficult to fully set up Magnezone ex.

While Oricorio performs well against Pokémon ex, it can struggle against non-ex decks due to its lower durability.
