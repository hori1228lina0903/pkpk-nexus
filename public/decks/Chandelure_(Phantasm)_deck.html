  1. In the early game, use Meloetta as a wall while playing a Trainer card every turn to maximize Chandelure’s attack effect.
  2. During the mid game, if the opponent’s main attacker is an ex Pokémon, bring in Oricorio or similar counters while continuing to play a Trainer card every turn to stabilize the board.
  3. In the late game, maximize Chandelure’s attack effect damage and close out the match in a single decisive push.

Deck Weaknesses

The deck heavily relies on Chandelure’s attack effect, so if Trainer card flow is disrupted, its damage output drops significantly.

If Meloetta is not drawn in the early game, the lack of a wall Pokémon makes the opening extremely difficult.

Because the deck relies on Oricorio as an answer to ex Pokémon, it can struggle against non-ex focused decks.
