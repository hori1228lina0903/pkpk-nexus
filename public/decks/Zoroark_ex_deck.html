  1. In the early game, use Zorua in the Active Spot and evolve into Zoroark ex using its attack.
  2. During the mid game, attack with Zoroark ex while preparing Mega Absol ex on the Bench.
  3. In the late game, use a second Zoroark ex or Mega Absol ex together with Darkrai ex’s 20 damage Ability to finish off opponents.

Deck Weaknesses

Zoroark ex can deal up to 90 damage with only 1 Energy in the early game, but the deck can struggle with damage output in the late game.

Since Zoroark ex’s damage depends on the number of Pokémon on your Bench, the deck can struggle when the Bench is not fully set up.
