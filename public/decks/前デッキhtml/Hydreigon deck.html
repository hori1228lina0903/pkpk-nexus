Deck Features

  • A well-balanced deck featuring poison, healing, sniping, and fast-paced high-damage attacks.
  • Focus on using Copycat aggressively to accelerate the setup of Hydreigon.
  • In the late game, overwhelm and knock out the opponent's main attackers with consecutive high-damage strikes from Hydreigon.
  • Since Hydreigon and Nihilego are non-ex Pokémon, they provide a strong advantage when playing against Oricorio.

Key Card Analysis

Mega Absol ex
  • Attacker & Disrupter.
  • From the early to mid-game, pressure the opponent with its 170 HP durability and a fast-paced 80-damage attack for two Energy.
  • Use Darkness Fang to look at the opponent's hand to plan your strategy, while disrupting their game by discarding Supporter cards.
Hydreigon
  • Main Attacker.
  • Knock out the opponent's main attackers with consecutive 130-damage strikes.
  • The key is whether you can complete its evolution quickly.
  • Use Copycat to accelerate and complete the evolution.
Copycat
  • Use this when you need Hydreigon or Rare Candy. Aim to complete the Hydreigon setup.

How to Play Hydreigon Deck

  1. Set up Hydreigon as fast as possible.
  2. Attack relentlessly with Hydreigon.
