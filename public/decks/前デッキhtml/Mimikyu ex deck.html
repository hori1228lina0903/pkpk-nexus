Deck Features

  • Use Mimikyu ex as a wall, utilizing its Ability to nullify the opponent's attack once while setting up Giratina ex and Greninja on the Bench to prepare for high-damage strikes.
  • Facilitate easy retreating with Mimikyu ex's Ability and Peculiar_Plaza, while accelerating Energy to get Giratina ex ready for battle.
  • Snive benched Pokémon with Greninja's "Water Shuriken" while using Cyrus to force out specific targets for Giratina ex to knock out in a single hit.
  • Since this is a control-oriented deck that requires time for setup, it tends to be vulnerable against fast-paced, high-aggression decks.

Key Card Analysis

Mimikyu ex
  • Acts as a wall with its "Mystical Guard" Ability, which nullifies the opponent's attack once.
  • Accelerates Psychic Energy while buying time for the rest of the team to set up.
  • Highly durable due to its high HP, allowing it to stay on the field longer.
Giratina ex
  • The deck's primary attacker.
  • Self-accelerates one Psychic Energy at the end of your turn with its "Abyssal Cry" Ability.
  • Can unleash "Chaos Impact" for 130 damage consecutively starting from the 3rd turn.
Greninja
  • Snipes the opponent's Bench with the "Water Shuriken" Ability.
  • Assists Giratina ex in securing one-hit KOs by utilizing Cyrus to force out specific targets.
Peculiar Plaza
  • Reduces the Retreat Cost of all Psychic Pokémon on both sides of the field by 2 Energy.
  • Facilitates easier switching for Mimikyu ex and Giratina ex, accelerating the deck's overall setup.

How to Play Mimikyu ex & Giratina ex Deck

  1. Deploy Mimikyu ex to the Active Spot and use its "Mystical Guard" Ability to nullify the opponent's attack once, buying time for your setup.
  2. Evolve Froakie into Greninja on the Bench to continuously accumulate 20–40 damage on the opponent's Benched Pokémon with "Water Shuriken."
  3. Once Giratina ex has 2 to 3 Psychic Energy attached via its "Abyssal Cry" Ability, move it to the Active Spot.
  4. Use Mysterious Wasteland to reduce Retreat Costs, then combine Greninja's sniping with Cyrus to force out the opponent's ace and knock it out with Giratina ex's "Chaos Impact."
  5. If Mimikyu ex is knocked out, redeploy another one to the field to cycle your attackers and sustain Giratina ex's high-damage offensive.
