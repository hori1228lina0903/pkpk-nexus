Deck Features

  • Balanced type with draw power, sniping, and fast-paced offense.
  • Deploy Suicune ex to the Active Spot in the early game to aim for the completion of Greninja / Greninja ex with its per-turn draw Ability.
  • Overwhelm opponents with a wide range of attacks: Suicune ex's maximum 140 damage, Greninja's sniping Ability, and Greninja ex's 2-Energy 100-damage attack.
  • The toughness of Greninja ex's 170 HP is also strong, allowing it to withstand hits from opponent attackers.

Key Card Analysis

Suicune ex
  • Wall & Attacker.
  • The draw power of the "Legendary Pulse" Ability and the high offensive capability of up to 140 damage are powerful.
  • Deploy to the Active Spot in the early game to aim for the completion of Greninja ex while drawing cards.
Greninja
  • Sniping Specialist: Capable of dealing 20 damage to any Pokémon, including those on the Bench, using its Ability.
  • Highly versatile, from sniping the opponent's Baby Pokémon or Pre-evolved Pokémon in the early game to damaging main attackers to bring them out with "Cyrus" and finishing them off with "Greninja ex."
Greninja ex
  • Main Attacker.
  • The quick attack capability of 100 damage for 2 Energy and the toughness of 170 HP to withstand at least one hit from the opponent's main attacker are excellent.
  • The ability to move to the Active Spot for zero Energy with the "Waterflow Change" Ability also creates synergy with "Suicune ex."

How to Play Suicune ex Deck

  1. Deploy Suicune ex to the Active Spot and use its Ability to draw cards.
  2. Prioritize completing the evolution of Greninja ex.
  3. When going first, use Suicune ex as a wall while attaching Energy to your Benched Pokémon. Use Greninja ex's Ability to switch and activate main attacker.
  4. Use the combination of Greninja and Cyrus to knock out the opponent's key Pokémon.
