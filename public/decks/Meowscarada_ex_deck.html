Deck Weaknesses

Since the deck needs to choose between two different evolution paths depending on the situation, it can be difficult to pilot efficiently.

Since the deck mainly relies on Meowscarada ex’s primary attack to apply pressure, damage reduction effects can significantly reduce its damage output.

The deck can struggle against high-damage decks such as Mega Blaziken ex.
