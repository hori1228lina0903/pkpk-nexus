Tier Decks

  • S Tier: A safe choice if you want results in tournaments or ranked matches. Boasts top-tier consistency.
  • A Tier: Depending on the meta, can go toe-to-toe with or even outperform S Tier. In certain metas, it can surpass top-tier decks.
  • B Tier: Solid usage and win rates, but somewhat less consistent compared to S Tier. However, certain decks have S-tier win rates by the numbers alone.
  • C Tier: Strong when the meta or predictions are in your favor, or has a high "fun/rogue deck" factor.
  • D Tier: Struggles in the current meta. Better suited for casual or friendly battles.
